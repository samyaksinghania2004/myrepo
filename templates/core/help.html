Help / FAQ

How do I find events?

Use the Events page to browse upcoming published events. You can filter by club, date and tag, or use the search page for keywords.

How do I join open rooms?

Open the Open Rooms page, choose a room, and pick an anonymous handle. Public rooms admit you immediately; private rooms require an invite.

What are anonymous handles and reveal actions?

Your handle is how others see you inside a room. Moderators can map handles back to real users internally. In serious cases, they may reveal the real identity behind a handle and expel that user from the room.

Can I change event registrations?

You can register or cancel while the event is still open and has not yet ended. If an event is full, you may be placed on a waitlist.
